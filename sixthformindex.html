Summer Term

This term we wish all of our Year 12 and 13 students the best of success in their examinations in May and June.

Year 12 complete their studies on May 13th and return to begin their second year topics on June 13th.

Year 13 complete their courses on May 27th and will enjoy their celebration lunch and then the Prom in the evening.

Post-16 Bursary Fund and Discretionary Awards

Kesgrave, along with all other schools with sixth forms, has been given the responsibility by government of providing discretionary bursary payments to some students. This fund has been designed by the Government to be available to those students most in need of financial support in post-16 education.

For more information please read the follow letters
